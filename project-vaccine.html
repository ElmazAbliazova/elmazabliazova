Redesigned Vaccine Carrier

Minimizing Cold Chain Disruption in Rural Uganda - Global Health Design Collaborative (Design Team)

Project Overview

Redesigned vaccine carrier to minimize cold chain disruption in rural Uganda. The project focused on maintaining vaccine efficacy during transport and storage in challenging field conditions.

Workstation Design

Designed inserted vial holders and dedicated workstation zones for vaccine preparation and administration. These features improved organization and supported standardized field procedures.

CAD Modeling

Created detailed 3D models and assemblies in SolidWorks to guide fabrication. Design iterations were informed by manufacturability and field usability constraints.

Thermal Performance Analysis

Conducted thermal analysis and experimental testing to compare insulation performance across multiple prototype iterations. Collected temperature data to evaluate design improvements.
